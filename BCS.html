B.C.S. Bachelor of Computer Science

Eligibility Criteria.


1. Any candidate who has passed H.SC. or equivalent to HSC. from science stream with English as a compulsory subject and have secured minimum 50% marks in HSC

2. The admission of the student will be finalized only after the university eligibility.
