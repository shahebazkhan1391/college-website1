B.Com(Bachelor of Commerce):-
In the faculty of Commerce, college offers three years full time degree course at undergraduate level.

Eligibility Criteria


1. For admission to first year B.Com, candidates must have passed HSC or equivalent to H. S. C. or D. Ed exam with English as a compulsory subject of 100 marks or Diploma in technical education conducted by Board of Technical Education with not Jess than 50%

2. Only those students who have perviously studied Arabic at junior level will be allowed to take Arabic as second language at B.Com first year course J The admission of the students will be finalized only after the university eligibility
